Who is the Team Mobility UK?

A new era of rapid change for bus services in Great Britain has begun. Transport Authorities are under pressure to deliver more effective bus strategies in new ways - from franchising to new approaches to working within the deregulated market. At the same time, the bus fleet is transitioning to zero emission.

With so much change happening so quickly, 'been there, done that' expertise is in short supply. Team Mobility UK is here to help.

Founded by Trans-Missions, a European public transport consultancy specialising in franchising, we have assembled a team of international and UK experts to help transport authorities navigate this new landscape. From planning to delivery, we draw upon decades of practical experience in working with local and regional transport authorities to design successful bus strategies and then deliver them.

Team Mobility UK has close links with the Bus Centre of Excellence (BCoE) since last summer, and team members attended the Quality Rural Bus conference in Hatfield and the Quality Bus conference in Portsmouth. Our experts have delivered three presentations in the last few months, on the topics of Bus Franchising practice in France, DRT in France and on the concept of free public transport in continental Europe. As active participants in the BCoE Bus Franchising Group, Team Mobility UK continues to bring valuable international perspectives to support BCoE's collective expertise and development.
