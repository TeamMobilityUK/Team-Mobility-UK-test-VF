Who is the Team Mobility UK?

A new era of rapid change for bus services in Great Britain has begun. Transport Authorities are under pressure to deliver more effective bus strategies in new ways - from franchising to new approaches to working within the deregulated market. At the same time, the bus fleet is transitioning to zero emission. With so much change happening so quickly, ‘been there, done that’ expertise is in short supply. Team Mobility UK is here to help. Founded by Trans-Missions, a European public transport consultancy specialising in franchising, we have assembled a team of international and UK experts to help transport authorities navigate this new landscape. From planning to delivery, we draw upon decades of practical experience in working with local and regional transport authorities to design successful bus strategies and then deliver them.

The Team Mobility UK is supported by the Bus Centre of Excellence of the Department for Transport.
